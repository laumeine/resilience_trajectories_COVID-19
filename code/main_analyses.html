Participants do not seem significantly more stressed during the pandemic than they were before - overall moderate levels of perceived stress (M = 21.06, SD = 3.52). Of 29 pandemic-specific stressors, participants experienced on average 10.5 (SD = 3.1) which they rated on a scale from 1 = “not at all burdensome” to 5 = “extremely burdensome”. On average stressor intensity was reported as “a bit burdensome” (M = 3.19, SD = 0.64). The first lockdown in Germany may not have had as negative an impact on students as later restrictions. However, we would need data from another follow-up to be sure.
